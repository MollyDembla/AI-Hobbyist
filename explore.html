Performance Measure

Performance measurement in AI evaluates how effectively a model achieves its intended purpose. Key metrics, such as accuracy, precision, recall, and F1-score, are used to assess the model's predictions. For continuous tasks, measures like mean squared error or R-squared gauge performance.

Model Creation

Model creation in AI involves designing and training algorithms to process data and solve specific problems. This process starts with selecting an appropriate model architecture, such as neural networks or decision trees, based on the task. Training the model requires feeding it data and adjusting its parameters to minimize errors and improve performance.
